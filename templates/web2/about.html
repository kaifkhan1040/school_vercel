About Us

LittleStar public school is basically for little kids from the age group of 2.5yrs to11.5 yrs and here we are ready to be our future leaders. Our school, established in2021, is centrally located in the hi-tech city Gurugram and is a standard for learning services management. Our School's aim is "Your child deserves the best" that we provide the best learning experience to all the students, Theschool promoted a wide array of Co-Curricular activities like- Music, Dance,Dramatics, Art and Craft etc. All Children are naturally curious. From Birthof a child's life, is the most critical learning period which becomes a reference for all future behavior, experiences and personality development.

Our Vision

  • To nurture confident, curious, and compassionate learners ready to face the challenges of tomorrow.
  • To create a joyful and inclusive environment that fosters holistic development in every child.
  • To be a leading institution known for innovation, excellence, and strong foundational education.

Our Mission

  • To provide a safe, engaging, and value-based learning atmosphere where children love to learn and grow.
  • To develop essential life skills, creativity, and discipline through a balanced academic and co-curricular program.
  • To build strong partnerships with parents and the community to support every child’s journey to success.
