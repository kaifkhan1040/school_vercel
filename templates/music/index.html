Accessible Organ

The organ below is monophonic. I created an updated version with full polyphony at https://codepen.io/sgestrella/pen/MNgGLq.

This organ keyboard uses the Web Audio API to generate organ tones. The HTML and CSS is designed with accessibility in mind. Those who cannot use a mouse may enjoy the organ using only keypresses or tab among the organ keys and press return to play the selected key. Created by Steven Estrella of ShearSpire Media.

  • Click each organ key. Click and hold to sustain the note.
  • Keyboard users may tab or shift-tab to each organ key and press return.
  • Or type qwertyuiop[] to play the white keys and 2356790= for the black keys.
  • Type the shift key to toggle the typing octave.
