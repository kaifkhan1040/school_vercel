Virtual drum kit

Drummers often use their right hand to play the cymbals (crash cymbal, ride cymbal, and hi-hat), and their left hand to play the snare drum. When using shortcuts, use the left hand to play both the snare drum and the bass drum. Play the virtual drums, and then apply your new skills to real drums. Use the drum machine to find inspiration for drum beats and rhythm patterns. A fun way to practice with the virtual drums is by playing along with your favorite songs.
